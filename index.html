TaskNest Privacy Policy

今办 · Package: com.aibang.jinban
Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

TaskNest is a lightweight to-do app. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not require an account and do not use your task content for ad targeting.

Data we store locally

  • Task titles, notes, priority levels, due dates, and reminders
  • App settings (theme, language, reminder toggle)

All of the above is kept in a local SQLite database on your device. There is no limit on active (incomplete) tasks.

Backup export

When you tap Export JSON in Settings, TaskNest writes a backup file to the system Downloads folder on Android (e.g. tasknest_export_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.json). You may delete these files in your file manager. Import lets you pick a JSON file from your device (merge or replace).

Data we do not collect (by TaskNest)

  • No user accounts or login
  • No server-side storage of your tasks (unless you export a JSON backup yourself)

Voice input

Voice controls appear in the task form only when speech is available on your device. When you use voice input, microphone access is requested.

RegionService
Mainland China (Android)Baidu Speech — always used (network required)
Other Android regionsSystem speech recognition (language packs may be required)
iOSSystem speech recognition

Audio is processed by the respective provider under their privacy policies. TaskNest does not operate a backend to store voice recordings.

Advertising

Today and Plan tabs display a Google AdMob banner at the bottom. Ads are served by Google LLC via AdMob and may use your device's advertising identifier for personalized or non-personalized ads. We do not use your task titles, notes, or other task content for ad targeting.

You can limit ad tracking in Android system settings. See Google's ads policy.

Done and Settings tabs do not show ads.

Children

TaskNest is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The in-app privacy policy and this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play store listing.
